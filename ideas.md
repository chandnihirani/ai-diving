### Trip sequencing idea

- **March 2025 – Kenya**  
  - Complete AOW and broaden skills beyond “just warm reef fun dives”.  
  - Adds **5–6 dives** → total ~14–15 dives.  

- **16–19 May 2025 – Mainland Sicily (coast)**  
  - Base yourself on the **eastern or north‑eastern coast** (still mainland Sicily), while the rest of the week you’re inland/central.  
  - Plan for **4–5 dives** over 2–3 days (e.g. 2× two‑tank days + optional 5th dive).  
  - Typical mid‑May sea temperature: **~18–19°C** → comfortable with **5–7 mm rental wetsuit + hood** (widely available at local dive centers).  
  - Good AOW‑friendly areas and example dive sites:  
    - **Taormina / Giardini Naxos area (east coast)**  
      - `Grotta Azzurra (Blue Grotto)`: cavern-style dive with beautiful light.  
      - `Isola Bella marine reserve`: walls with sponges, typical Med life (groupers, morays, octopus).  
      - `Capo Taormina / Roman columns`: easy archaeology-style dive with ancient ruins.  
      - `Blue Cave` / `Blue Grotto` variants around Capo Taormina (ask for a cavern/light‑effects dive).  
      - `Air Cave` / `Air Cavern` and similar small swim‑throughs (easy overhead environments).  
    - **When emailing Taormina centres, example sites to request**  
      - “Grotta Azzurra / Blue Grotto”  
      - “Isola Bella marine reserve walls”  
      - “Capo Taormina with Roman columns / archaeology site”  
      - “One cavern/cave‑style dive such as Blue Cave or Air Cave, if conditions allow”  
      - **Dive centres to contact**:  
        - [Blue Sea Diving Center](https://blueseadiving.it/) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Blue+Sea+Diving+Center+Taormina+Mazzaro)  
        - [Sea Spirit Diving Resort](https://www.seaspirit.it/en/) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sea+Spirit+Diving+Resort+Giardini+Naxos)  
        - [Taormina Diving Center](https://www.divingtaormina.it/?lang=en) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Taormina+Diving+Center+Giardini+Naxos)  
    - **Aci Trezza / Cyclops Islands marine area (near Catania)**  
      - Volcanic rock formations, swim‑throughs, and good chance of schooling fish and morays.  
    - **Cefalù (north coast)**  
      - Rocky reefs and walls; less “famous” by name, but solid Med reef diving via local centers.  

- **Early July 2025 – Europe trip (SECOND trip post-AOW)**  
  - Destination: **Malta**  
    - Plan for **6–8 dives** over the trip.  
    - Good match for a fresh AOW diver building on Kenya + Sicily experience.  
    - Introduces **wrecks, arches/swim‑throughs and caverns** with manageable conditions.  
    - Nice progression from Kenya reefs → Sicily coastal sites → Malta wrecks/caves.  
    - **Example sites to mention when emailing (so centres know what you’re aiming for)**  
      - **Cirkewwa area (northwest Malta)**  
        - `Rozi` tugboat wreck (≈30–35 m; classic AOW/deep profile).  
        - `P29` patrol boat wreck (similar depth range, often paired with Rozi/Cirkewwa reef).  
        - `Cirkewwa Arch` / `Right Arch` – natural arch / swim‑through (the “hole” you may have seen in photos).  
        - Cirkewwa reef & wall / plateau, with drop‑offs and smaller swim‑throughs.  
      - **Paradise Bay / adjacent reefs** – shallower, scenic reef dives; good balance day with less depth stress.  
      - **Stretch/conditions‑dependent options (only if the centre says appropriate for your experience + weather)**  
        - `Um El Faroud` wreck (deep, iconic but more advanced – only if they’re happy with your profile).  
        - Optional Gozo day‑trip to `Blue Hole` at Dwejra (famous vertical chimney + arch swim‑through) if they run it and conditions are good.  
    - **Areas & dive centres to consider (Malta north/east coast)**:  
      - **St Paul’s Bay / Bugibba area** – good base for shore and boat access to classic Malta wrecks and reefs.  
        - [Buddies Dive Cove](https://buddiesmalta.com/) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Buddies+Dive+Cove+Bugibba+Malta)  
        - [Maltaqua](https://www.maltaqua.com/) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Maltaqua+St+Paul%27s+Bay+Malta)  
        - [DiveBase Malta](https://divebasemalta.com/) – [Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=DiveBase+Malta+Bugibba)  

- **Mid–end October 2025 – Europe / Mediterranean trip**  
  - Aim for **another Med destination (not Malta)**, e.g.:  
    - **Cyprus** (Larnaca/Protaras – warmest late‑season Med, famous wrecks like Zenobia).  
    - **Southern Greece** (Crete, Rhodes) or **southern Italy/Sicily**.  
    - **Southern Spain** (e.g. Costa del Sol / Almería) depending on conditions.  
  - Plan for **4–6 dives**.  
  - Water will be cooler but manageable with **5–7 mm rental wetsuits + hood** (you’re happy using whatever thickness shops provide).  

- **Late 2025 / early 2026**  
  - Adjust next destinations based on what you enjoy most from Malta + October Med (reefs vs wrecks vs “adventure” profiles).  

- **2026–2027**  
  - Keep a loose rhythm of **2–3 trips per year**, adjusting for budget/time/energy.  
  - Choose trips for **variety and progression** (new locations, new conditions, new types of dives) rather than chasing a specific total‑dives number.
