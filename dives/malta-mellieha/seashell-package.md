## Reviews — equipment, carrying kit, logistics

*No public reviews found that specifically complain about lugging kit on shore dives. Several sources highlight the opposite for boat days and general logistics.*

### Centre / boat (official)

From [boat charter page](https://www.seashelldivingmalta.com/boat-charter) — why they push boat diving:

> **No walking with your heavy kit on your back**  
> **No climbing on the boat with the kit on. Easy and stress-free boarding by lift**

Hard-hull boat (2018): toilet, shade, **diver lift**. RIB also available. **4 of your 10 dives** are boat — no shore walk with kit for those days.

From [packages page](https://www.seashelldivingmalta.com/diving-packages-and-prices):

> Our **fleet of vehicles** makes your transfers between dive sites **efficient and comfortable**.

From [who we are](https://www.seashelldivingmalta.com/who-we-are):

> Custom-built hard boat with **lift for divers**, ensuring the experience is **comfortable, convenient and totally stress-free**.

### Sample shore-day flow (official holiday itinerary)

From [divingholidaymalta.com](https://www.divingholidaymalta.com/):

> We will **pick you up from your hotel at 08:00** … If you do not have your own equipment, we can **kit you out** with all the gear you need! You will be back at the dive center by about **13:30** ready for an afternoon of relaxing.

Shore days still involve **entry/exit in kit** at the site (e.g. Cirkewwa), but **van transport** to/from the site is included — you are not walking from Mellieha with tanks.
