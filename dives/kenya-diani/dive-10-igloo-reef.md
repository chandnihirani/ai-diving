## Reflections

First time doing a descent with a rope—I usually descend directly down, so this was interesting. The 6 kg weight belt was perfect; I didn't have any issues with floating upwards halfway through the dive and was stable the entire time. I usually have that as an issue, so this felt like a good sign. Overall it was a good dive: I was very confident, wasn't overthinking anything, and enjoyed the compass activity.
